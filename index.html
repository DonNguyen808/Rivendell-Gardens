Our main attraction is the koi fish pond

These koi fishes were imported over from Japan and they circle all around the garden. You can feed them and enjoy their company in this nice and relaxing atmosphere

Our Garden is free to tour

Before you reach to the koi fish pond, you first have to get through the garden. You are able to enjoy a nice walk throughout our garden that is split into 5 sections. The real magic starts when it gets darker and our beautiful lights completely changes and enhances the atmosphere.

  • Learn More

We also provide fruits and fruit trees

Before we built our amazing garden for people to enjoy. We have over a thousand fruit trees planted with many variety of fruits to pick from all year round. Schedule a time and day to come out and pick your own fruits in this fun activity. You will be able to purchase not only fruits, but also your very own fruit tree if you desire one in your backyard. All trees are air layered and grafted which will fruit in 1 to 2 years.
